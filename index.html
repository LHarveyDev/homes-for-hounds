Homes for Hounds

About Us

Homes for hounds are a charity dedicated to the re-homing of retired racing greyhounds. These dogs settle into retirement easily and make wonderful pets.

If you are considering re-homing a dog then why not speak to us about our greyhounds. We have dogs suitable for all types of households including those with children and other animals.

Greyhounds are easy to keep, they require surprisingly little exercise and are quiet and clean in the house. They have a naturally calm, laid-back temperament and adapt from life on the track to life on the couch with ease.

Did you know the greyhound is one of the oldest breeds in history? These noble dogs have been traced back over 4,000 years to early cave drawings and are the only breed mentioned in the Bible. By giving a greyhound a home, you are taking on a true thoroughbred and a breed that has been “man's best friend” for thousands of years!

Facts & Figures
  • Height: Male - 71-76cm, Female - 69-71cm
  • Weight: Male - 27-40KG, Female - 26-34KG
  • Average Retirement Age: Between 3 - 5 years of age
  • Average Lifespan: Up to 15 years!
  • Exercise Required: Perfectly happy with two twenty minute walks a day Greyhounds are built for speed, not stamina, and use up their energy in short bursts.
  • Temperament - Intelligent, Gentle, Affectionate & Even Tempered.
